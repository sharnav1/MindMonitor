First Aid During A Seizure

Follow these steps to help someone during a seizure:

1. Stay Calm

Keep calm and reassure others. Do not panic.

2. Protect the Head

Place something soft under the person's head to prevent injury.

3. Turn to the Side

Once the seizure stops, roll the person onto their side.

4. Do Not Put Anything In The Mouth

Never put objects like a cloth, spoon, etc. in the person's mouth during a seizure.

First Aid for Absence Seizures

Follow these important steps:

1. Stay Calm

Absence seizures usually last only a few seconds.

2. Do Not Shake or Yell

Do not try to wake them up or shout during the seizure.

3. Ensure Safety

If the person is walking or in a risky place, gently guide them to safety.

4. Repeat Missed Information

After the seizure, calmly explain anything they missed.
